Work-In-Progress

This is an extremely early version of a 2D sidescroller. I aim to continue working on it in my spare time to experiment with various game systems and mechanics, and to learn about game design as a whole.

Note: Player and Item animations are currently low priority and largely incomplete. Any animation seen is strictly for testing.

Controls

  • E: Spawn Enemy
  • B: inventory
  • U: unequip all gear
  • Left Click: Attack/Equip Item
  • Right Click: Pick up item (yellow squares)
  • Spacebar: Jump/Wall Jump

Current Goals:

  • Character Controls
    • Intuitive moveset
    • 3-step attack combo
    • Feels good to control
  • Simple enemy which moves toward the player, and attacks when within range
  • Testing level
